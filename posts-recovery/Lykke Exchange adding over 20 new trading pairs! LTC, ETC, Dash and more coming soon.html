App UI redesigned

Furthermore they have redesigned the app with version 5.6 / 12.8.4 The homepage in the app is redesigned in accordance with the new Lykke guidelines. Now it shows the total balance of all your assets. Trading wallet, Private wallet and Margin wallet with the total balance for each one are shown below.

Some other important changes include:

Depositing is now easier than ever. You can deposit any of three wallets with a simple tap on the blue plus icon next to it. Editing a placed limit order is now available, all you need to do is select existing order, tap edit in limit order details, change parameters and confirm the action. Your changes will apply immediately. BTC deposits are now displayed on the balance much faster. Due to the change in algorithms, the scan time was reduced from several hours to several minutes. Now you can see BTC deposit in your wallet after just three confirmations on the blockchain."
